Age Restriction


As you know, age restriction is becoming a problem now. Since you are all anonymous, we have no max and minimum to the age restriction.

So we've made "AgeSelector". When writing a book, choosing the audience restrictions is required for the safety of others.


Some are in the following:


G (Everyone)

PG (Parental Guidance)

13+ (13 and over)

14+ (14 and over)


More info on this here.


We have removed MA and R for the safety of underage users.
